## Experience

My career started at Notch, a Richmond-based software consultancy where I built full-stack web applications for various clients. In 2017, Capital One acquired Notch and brought the team into their Center for Machine Learning. Over the next six years I grew from data engineer to Lead ML Engineer (IC), building Python tools that served data scientists across the enterprise. In 2024 I made a deliberate move toward AI-native work, leading GenAI pipeline projects at Vectari AI, taking on select consulting engagements, and ultimately joining Blueberry AI as Head of Engineering.

### Blueberry AI - Head of Engineering (Mar 2025 – Present)

At Blueberry AI I built an AI-powered platform to help hiring teams evaluate candidates more consistently by turning interview data into structured, role-specific assessments. As Head of Engineering, I architected and productionized the company’s core candidate assessment platform, designing a multi-agent pipeline of ~75 agents using LangChain, LangGraph, Prefect, PydanticAI, and Pydantic. The system analyzed interview evidence against open role requirements, generated structured hiring recommendations, and served as a production decision-support tool for recruiters and hiring managers.
